Welcome to WebWorld!

Choose and Customize an Avatar

To choose an avatar, touch the Choose Avatar button. Touch an avatar image to select it. To customize an avatar, press Customize.

Choose a World

There are many different worlds in WebWorld. To select a world, press Choose World and move through the worlds to choose. Some worlds are games, with scoring or levels. Other worlds are just for building, exploring and playing.

Playing

To start playing with the chosen avatar and world, press Play. You may need to wait a bit until the world initializes.

Changing View

You can change the view of a world by repeatedly touching the View button. Four different views are available.

Pause/Resume

Touch the Pause button to pause the world. When paused, you can use your fingers to zoom and rotate your view. You can then take a screen snapshot to record the action!

Moving

There are three ways to move your avatar:

  1. Tilt - to turn the avatar, tilt left and right. To move forward and backward, tilt front and back.
  2. Touch - use the large avatar button on the bottom left or right. Touch and drag on it up, down, left and right to move and turn.
  3. Controller - Web World supports most game controllers. You can also use the keyboard on a Chromebook. See gallantrealm.com for help on using controllers.

Levels

Many game worlds have levels to move up in where the game gets harder. To reset the level, use the Reset Level button on the Choose World page.

Enjoy!
